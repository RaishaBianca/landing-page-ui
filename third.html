Mobile Marketplace

Study Case

Create a mobile design for a marketplace

Research methode :Competitor Analysis

Duration : 1 Day

Role : UI Designer, UX Researcher

Problems & Solutions

Problems

  • Home menu that can attract customers to the product and easy to navigate
  • Products menu that is easy to navigate and can show the customers the products according to their requirements
  • Product details menu to showcase the product details that's easy to understand

Solutions

  1. Home Menu
    • Banner ads to promote current product sales and attract customers.
    • Category section to easily navigate through products by category
    • Product showcase by promo best promo and best sellers to attract users to all the current promo and popular products
    • Product showcase according to user's preference
    • Search, cart, and chat in the navigation bar for easier navigation and notification
  2. Products Menu
    • Sort and filter features so users can easily find what type of product they're looking for
    • product showcase in the form of infinite scrolling
  3. Details Menu
    • Product image to make it easier to see the product
    • product detaila
    • Shop that provided the product so users can become potential loyal customers
    • Other product by the store
    • Product showcase according to user's preference
